About Nanna

Nanna Ditzel (1923–2005) was a Danish furniture designer and architect. She played a significant role in the mid-20th-century Scandinavian design movement.

Some of her notable designs include the Trinidad chair, which became an iconic piece of Danish modern furniture. Her work has had a lasting impact on the world of design, and she is celebrated for her contributions to the mid-century modern design movement.
